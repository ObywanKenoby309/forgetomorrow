FORGETOMORROW MEDIA TEXT & COLOR GUIDELINES

1. Brand Colors (Hex codes only)

Steel Azure Dark → #112033 (primary background / headers)

Orange Accent → #FF7043 (buttons, logos, CTAs)

Hover Orange → #F4511E (hover states)

Gray Main → #D1D5DB (body text)

Gray Secondary → #9CA3AF (secondary text, captions)

White → #FFFFFF (text on dark backgrounds, highlights)

2. Text Guidelines

Headers / Titles: White (#FFFFFF) or Orange (#FF7043) on dark backgrounds.

Body Text: Gray Main (#D1D5DB) on dark backgrounds; Gray Secondary (#9CA3AF) for captions.

Buttons / CTAs: Orange Accent (#FF7043) with White text; hover state #F4511E.

Links: Orange Accent (#FF7043), hover #F4511E.

3. Accessibility Notes

Text over Steel Azure Dark must be #FFFFFF or #FF7043 for contrast.

Small text should avoid orange to remain legible.
